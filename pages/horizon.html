What is BVR Horizon?

BVR Horizon gives BVR skin in the game. We back ventures with strategic value — whether homegrown, externally sourced, or acquired — and help them grow using the full capacity of our studio network.

When we see ideas with real potential — we don't just advise. We step in. We build. We scale.

What We Do

We're not a fund. We're a builder. Our investment model is operational, strategic, and hands-on.

Strategic Venture Investment

We take equity positions in ventures that align with BVR's innovation roadmap, adding value through execution support and strategic partnerships.

Venture Co-Building

We work with founders to co-create or expand ideas — contributing our product teams, market access, or IP.

Acquisitions & Turnarounds

We acquire businesses with untapped potential and retool them for future markets.

Venture Enablement

Portfolio support through branding, tech, data, go-to-market, and capital readiness — powered by other BVR units.

How We Work

Our investment model is operational, strategic, and hands-on:

1. Pipeline Discovery

We identify ventures through accelerators, partnerships, internal scouting, or direct pitch.

2. Fit Assessment

We look for strategic alignment — can this venture benefit from BVR's platforms, IP, or execution power?

3. Deal Structure

Flexible engagement: equity, convertible notes, revenue share, or co-build partnerships.

4. Venture Activation

Plug the venture into BVR's network — project support, tech teams, strategy, or growth design.

5. Scale & Exit Readiness

Prepare ventures for follow-on investment, scale, or sustainable exits.

What We Look For

  • ● Execution-ready ventures needing strategy, structure, or market traction
  • ● Niche businesses in sectors where BVR is already active (climate, agri-tech, creative, digital infrastructure)
  • ● Under-leveraged assets (e.g., brands, IP, distribution) with growth potential
  • ● Founders with vision, and a willingness to work with a hands-on partner
